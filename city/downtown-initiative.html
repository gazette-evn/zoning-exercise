What We Heard From You

Over the past eight months, we've gathered input from thousands of Riverside residents through town halls, focus groups, and surveys. Your priorities are clear:

  • More housing options in downtown
  • Walkable, mixed-use neighborhoods
  • Preservation of historic character
  • Expanded parks and green space

The Proposal

Based on community feedback, the Planning Department recommends a new mixed-use zoning overlay for approximately 12 blocks of the downtown core. Key elements include:

  • Increased residential density to meet housing demand
  • Ground-floor retail with housing above
  • Reduced parking requirements to encourage walking and transit
  • Height increases with step-back requirements
  • Affordable housing incentive program
