About Meridian

Meridian Development Group is a leading real estate development firm specializing in transformative mixed-use projects that revitalize urban cores and create vibrant, walkable communities.

Founded in 2008, we've partnered with municipalities across Arizona and the Southwest to deliver projects that balance economic growth with community needs.

Our approach combines deep market expertise with collaborative partnerships that ensure every project reflects the unique character of its community.
